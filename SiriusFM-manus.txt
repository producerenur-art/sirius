SiriusFM – manus for ElevenLabs (engelsk kvinnestemme)
=======================================================

Lim inn teksten under i ElevenLabs. La linjeskiftene stå – de gir
naturlige pauser mellom scenene.

-------------------------------------------------------
SiriusFM. Free radio, always on. Non-stop electronic music — anytime, anywhere, free.

Upload your sound. Share your own tracks and DJ mixes with listeners worldwide.

A living magazine. An AI-powered magazine and radio, refreshed around the clock.

Your frequency awaits. Listen now, at sirius F M dot no.
-------------------------------------------------------

TIPS:
- Skriv "sirius F M dot no" (med mellomrom) så leser hun det tydelig.
- Anbefalte stemmer: Rachel, Jessica, Sarah, Aria eller Matilda.
- Innstillinger: Stability ~45 %, Similarity ~75 %, Style lav.
- Last ned som MP3 og legg fila på skrivebordet (f.eks. "stemme.mp3").
